*Challenges of property:* 

* *minimal sun/lots of shade,*   
* *steep or sloping terrain,*   
* *Needs to be low maintenance,* 

*Variety of gardens & produce:* 

* *Vegetables, herbs and fruit trees*   
* *Wide range of medicinal & culinary herbs*   
* *Orchard for fruit, nut,*   
* *Significant native planting*  
* *Wide range of subtropical trees*  
* *Food forest (mixed)*

* *Hot composting set-up*  
* *Cold compost (add to regularly),*  
* *Worm farm/in-ground tunnel,*
